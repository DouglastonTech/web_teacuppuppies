Golden Retriever
This dog is the golden retriever so if you want this dog just know that it is large and athletic. It is very cheerful and it moves its tail back and forth naturally. (So it doesn't always mean it is happy.) This dog is also responsive to training. It is also peaceful to your other pets. But you need to exercise with your Golden Retriever. They jump a lot especially when they are young. It sheds a lot! Make sure to brush its hair to avoid mats and tangles.
